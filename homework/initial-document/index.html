Weather Site Planning

Audience

For this website the audience will be anyone that is in the cities of Franklin, ID; Greenvile, SC; and Springfield, IL that are in need of checking the weather outside in order to plan activities with the families or friends. This website's audience's age can range from 12+ years old. Internet access is a must.

Purpose Statement

In order to provide a good service for all outdoor activities we offer you an all-time available weather service so that you may take that pretty girl on a date or your friends to the dunes knowing that it will not rain that day.
